Thank you for visiting Betty and Barbara’s Basketball Bonanza

Betty and Barbara’s Basketball Bonanza was founded in 2000 four years after sisters Betty and Barbara graduated from Purdue University. Both sisters loved playing basketball growing up and were even good enough to both play on the Purdue Women's Basketball team.

Betty and Barbara are committed to bringing you the best basketball equipment and other related items the world has to offer.

Our Story

Starting in a small building just 15 minutes from Purdue University's campus Betty and Barbara first started hosting basketball camps and selling a few related items. Over the years, Betty and Barbara’s Basketball Bonanza has grown into a trusted brand in the basketball community. In addition to training caps now we also supply high-quality basketballs, apparel, and training gear to athletes of all levels. Betty and Barbara are committed to growing the game specifically here in Indiana, as a result Betty and Barbara’s Basketball Bonanza is the official provider of gear to Purdue University, Indiana University, The Indiana Pacers and Indiana Fever.
